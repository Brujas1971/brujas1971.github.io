Designed by emerging artists VINNY6NAMES of Nocturnal Sons Posse and Robin Giordani of BRUJAS / The Corner Society, the initial run is inspired by prison rebels, deviants, and the brujas of the past, present and future. 1971 was inspired by the events that occurred on September 9, 1971. 1,000 prisoners, most of them black and brown, rioted and seized control of Attica prison in New York. After four days of negotiations and taking hostages, authorities agreed to 28 of the prisoners' demands such as better medical treatment, fair visitation rights, and an end to physical brutality. 45 years later, prisoners launched a national work strike demanding an end to prison slavery, which entered its second consecutive month during the launch of the project, October, 2016.
